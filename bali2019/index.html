Why a retreat?

We are all gathering in Phuket for on week to work together in better enviroment. These are not holidays, see it as a more relaxed company time in a different environment.

The main goal of the retreat is to get to know the whole team. Make sure you do not always stay with your usual collegues and mingle. Do not be afraid, none of them bite!

We will also have activites, some optional, some mandatory. Come and join as many as you want and let's have a blast!

Make sure to let the Operations team know if you need anything. We are not hear to nurse you, but we can try to help!

FAQ

A few things that are important for you to make the stay of the team as awesome as it can.

What costs are covered by the team?

  • Bus (Oct 24, office to airport)
  • Flight (Shanghai - Bali, Bali - Jakarta - Shanghai)
  • Travel insurance
  • Hotel & Breakfast (Oct 25 - Nov 2)
  • Team building & Team lunch (Oct 26)
  • Team building & Team lunch (Oct 27)
Everything else is not covered.

Do I need a visa to visit Indonesia?

No. Otherwise Ops team would have already reached out to you to apply for one. :)

What documents should I bring upon entry into Indonesia?

  • Passport with minimum validity of 6 months
  • A return ticket or through ticket
  • Proof of accommodation arrangements
  • Disembarkation card for foreigner
Digital and print copy of item 2 and 3 will be provided by Ops team in the following days.
Item 4 should be handed out onboard or be found in Bali airport.

What’s the currency of Indonesia? How can I exchange?

Indonesian rupiah (Rp, IDR) is used in Indonesia. IDR can not be exchanged from CNY in China, so please prepare enough USD in advance and exchange to IDR in Indonesia. Otherwise, you can use your UnionPay debit card to withdraw IDR on ATM.

What plugs and sockets are used in Indonesia?

Indonesia uses European-style two-pin round plugs. Please bring adaptors.

Is travel insurance needed?

It's covered by the team.

Where can I buy a SIM card?

You can order already on Taobao (e.g. this link), or purchase at the airport or in shopping malls.

Where will we work during the retreat?

It is up to each team leader to decide where the team will work during the retreat.

Do I need to tip in Indonesia? How much do I tip?

Tipping is not mandatory but expected in service industries. If you’d like to tip, 5-10% of the total bill is appropriate.
