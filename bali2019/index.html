Why a retreat?

We are all gathering in Phuket for on week to work together in better enviroment. These are not holidays, see it as a more relaxed company time in a different environment.

The main goal of the retreat is to get to know the whole team. Make sure you do not always stay with your usual collegues and mingle. Do not be afraid, none of them bite!

We will also have activites, some optional, some mandatory. Come and join as many as you want and let's have a blast!

Make sure to let the Operations team know if you need anything. We are not hear to nurse you, but we can try to help!

FAQ

A few things that might be important for you and that we would need you to follow to make the stay of the team as awesome as we can.

Do not forget your passport

Make sure you bring your passport as it will be needed for the plan and the hotel. It is also very handy to have a photo of your passpot and visa in your phone and leave your passport at your hotel.

Be respectful of others

It is a different country, culture and customs. Make sure you are respectful, patient and show genuine interest in others. If there are any troubles, just step away and come back to the hotel while communicating your location and the issue to others.

Do not carry too much cash or many valuables with you!

If you are going to the beach, for a visit or to a bar, do not carry too much with you. Take enough money with you to cover your expenses but do not carry all of your belongings with you.

What to do if there is a major problem?

Stay calm, get in a safe place and call for help. Depending on the emergency contact one of the following you might want to contact the police, emergency or the hotel.

Who pays for the activities?

On Tuesday and Thursday the company will pay for the activities. The rest of the days you will have free time and be able to do things on your own.

Can we bring our partners?

No. The point of the retreat is to meet and strengthen the bonds between team members.

Which meals are included?

Breakfast every day. Lunch on Tuesday and Thursday. Dinner in Saturday and Thursday.

Do we receive pocket money?

Please bring your own pocket money depending on your needs and activities you want to do. Their government requires that tourist should at least take 20,000 THB or the same value foreign currency, it is about 4,000 CNY. Customs will randomly check, but it is not common, usually they will check people with visa on arrival. Not required for German office as we do not need a visa.

Who is taking care of the Visa (only Chinese citizens)?

Your beloved Operations Team. If you did not received anything, let them know!

What time is the breakfast?

The breakfast runs from 06:00am to 11:00 am. The waiter will ask for your room number on arrival.

What is included (breakfast, lunch, dinner) in the hotel service?

Only the breakfast is. If any additional cost is made on a room it will be at your charge.

Who pays transportation tickets in Phuket?

On the days of common activities, the company pays the tickets to go to the activities. Freetime transport will not be covered.

How do we get to downtown?

By Taxi in 10 min or by shuttle bus from the hotel.

When is the room cleaning?

The cleaning service will clean the rooms meanwhile we are out. You can also put the sign.

Is there a supermarket near the the hotel?

There is a small grocery store inside the hotel.

Which facilities does the hotel have?

Swimming pool opened 8 AM to 6 PM and a gym opened 7 AM to 8 PM.

Where can I buy a sim card and how much does a data sim card cost?

Sim Cards are available at the airport or in shopping malls. 8 days around 300 Baht.

Is a travel insurance needed?

Can be optionally purchased. Wiredcraft is not covering the cost for that.

Is there a doctor at the hotel?

24h medical service can be found near the hotel.
